Abstract

We propose Vector Deformable Fields (VDF), a novel approach for high-fidelity 4D reconstruction from monocular videos. Our method learns a continuous spatio-temporal representation that captures both geometric and appearance variations over time. By modeling deformations as vector fields in a canonical space, VDF achieves state-of-the-art performance in reconstructing dynamic scenes with complex motions and topological changes.
